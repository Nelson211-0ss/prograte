About Prograte Capital Ventures

Revolutionizing financial inclusion for women entrepreneurs in Uganda's informal sector

🚀

Our Mission

To empower Ugandan women entrepreneurs through targeted micro-investments and support, driving economic growth and gender equality in the informal sector.

🔭

Our Vision

To be the leading catalyst for women's economic empowerment in Uganda, creating a thriving ecosystem of successful women-owned businesses that contribute significantly to the country's economic development.

Our Story

The journey of Prograte Capital Ventures from vision to impact

Early 2023

Identifying the Gap

Our founders recognized the critical financing gap for women entrepreneurs in Uganda's informal sector, who despite running profitable businesses, were systematically excluded from traditional financing options due to collateral requirements and formal documentation.

May 2023

Company Launch

Prograte Capital Ventures was officially established with a mission to revolutionize access to capital for women entrepreneurs through an innovative dividend-based investment model, beginning operations in Jinja, Uganda.

August 2023

First 100 Entrepreneurs

Within three months of launch, we had successfully deployed investments to our first 100 women entrepreneurs, validating our model and demonstrating the immense demand for our innovative financing approach.

October 2023

Expansion to Matugga-Kampala

Following our initial success, we established our second branch in Matugga-Kampala, extending our reach to more women entrepreneurs in Uganda's urban centers and refining our business support services.

January 2024

Busia Branch Opening

To serve entrepreneurs in border communities, we launched our third branch in Busia, creating new opportunities for cross-border traders and further diversifying our portfolio.

Present

400+ Businesses and Growing

Today, we've successfully invested in over 400 women-owned businesses across our three locations, with 74% remaining actively engaged and 26% achieving financial independence. Our $68,000 investment portfolio continues to generate consistent monthly returns of 5-10%.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our work and shape our approach

⚖️

Financial Inclusion

We believe everyone deserves access to fair and equitable financial services, regardless of their formal status or collateral ownership.

👩‍💼

Women's Empowerment

We recognize the transformative power of investing in women. When women entrepreneurs succeed, families prosper, communities thrive, and economies grow. Our focus on women-owned businesses is strategic and impactful.

🤝

Partnership Approach

We see our relationship with entrepreneurs as a partnership, not just a transaction. Our success is tied to their success, and we work collaboratively to ensure sustainable growth and mutual benefit.

🌱

Sustainable Growth

We prioritize long-term, sustainable growth over quick returns. Our approach nurtures businesses to develop strong foundations, ensuring lasting impact for entrepreneurs, investors, and communities.

💡

Innovation

We continuously innovate to address the unique challenges of Uganda's informal sector. Our dividend-based model represents a fundamental rethinking of how capital can flow to underserved entrepreneurs.

📊

Data-Driven Impact

We measure what matters. Our rigorous approach to tracking both financial and social metrics ensures transparency, accountability, and continuous improvement in our impact.
